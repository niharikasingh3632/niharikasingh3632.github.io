Hi! I work in Computer Vision and Vision–Language Models, with a focus on real-time object tracking and vision agents.

As an AI Engineer at [QpiAI](https://qpiai.tech/), I'm part of the AI products team building [QpiAI PRO](https://www.qpiai-pro.tech/), an AutoML and MLOps platform. I translate broad requirements into technical designs and turn AI research into functional features, spanning high-throughput pipelines, roadmap planning, and competitive analysis.

I hold a B.Tech in Computer Science & AI with minor in Entrepreneurship from [IIIT Delhi](https://www.iiitd.ac.in/convocation2025/StudentCorner.html). During my time there, I Co-founded and served as the first President of the [International Game Developer Association student chapter](https://igda.org/chapters/india-iiit-delhi/) and was a founding member of [CyFuse](https://cyfuse.iiitd.edu.in/#/home), a student club focused on AI applications. I also served on the university Sports Council. I also did undergraduate research on AI-based lung disorder detection at the [Translational Biology Laboratory](https://dhanjal-lab.iiitd.edu.in/) under Dr. [Jaspreet Kaur Dhanjal](https://www.iiitd.ac.in/jaspreet).

Outside work, I track AI research, explore new papers, and test model architectures, which is what I post about here.
